## The classes

**Wizards** — by the color of what they do:

| Color | Does | Who wears it |
|-------|------|--------------|
| ⚪ White | healing | Croaker (the physician) |
| ⚫ Black | destruction | One-Eye · Goblin |
| 🔴 Red | both | Silent |
| 🟣 Purple | all + shadow — **the Taken** | The Lady · Soulcatcher · The Limper |

Then the company roles:

- **Command** — the braid and the banner (the Lieutenant · Darling, the White Rose)
- **The Annalist** — the witness, the record (Croaker · Murgen · Sleepy)
- **Comedy Relief** — the feud that never ends (One-Eye & Goblin)
- **Fixers** — the ones who simply do it (Raven)
- **Undying Loyalty** — the old hands (Elmo · Otto & Hagop)
- **The Captain** — *the title that became a name* — set apart

## The roll

### ⚪ Wizard · White — healing
- **Croaker** — Company physician; the cutter who keeps them breathing. (Also the Annalist and, later, the Captain.)

### ⚫ Wizard · Black — destruction
- **One-Eye** — ancient wizard in the battered black hat; the poisoned spear; half the Company's magic and most of its trouble.
- **Goblin** — toad-faced sorcerer; One-Eye's eternal rival; deadly between insults.

### 🔴 Wizard · Red — both
- **Silent** — the mute sorcerer; speaks only in spellwork; heals and harms without a word.

### 🟣 Wizard · Purple — all + shadow · the Taken
- **The Lady** — sorceress-empress, heir to the Domination; near-absolute power; later Croaker's.
- **Soulcatcher** — one of the Ten Who Were Taken; many voices, one beautiful madness.
- **The Limper** — a Taken; cruel, relentless, hard to keep dead.

### 🎖️ Command — the braid and the banner
- **The Lieutenant** — the Captain's second; the steady hand between battles.
- **Darling** — deaf girl who becomes the **White Rose**; null to all sorcery; the banner reborn and the cause the Company comes to serve.

### 🖋️ The Annalist — the witness, the record
- **Croaker** — narrator and keeper of the Annals; physician; later Captain. The Company's memory: *if it isn't in the Annals, it didn't happen.*
- **Murgen** — standard-bearer turned Annalist; carried the banner and the record both.
- **Sleepy** — later Annalist and Captain; carried the Company through the Glittering Stone years.

### 😂 Comedy Relief — the feud that never ends
- **One-Eye & Goblin** — centuries of pranks, curses, and insults; the Company's running joke, and its deepest grief when it ends.

### Fixers — the ones who simply do it
- **Raven** — cold, methodical, lethal; Darling's protector; the man who does what must be done and says nothing after.

### Undying Loyalty — the old hands
- **Elmo** — sergeant; the steady core; the Company's spine.
- **Otto & Hagop** — the two who always come back, together and grumbling and alive.

### 🩸 The Captain — *the title that became a name*
- **The Captain** — known by no other name. The original commander; he held the Company together through its hardest years and, when he fell, passed the title to Croaker.

## An honest note on the Captain

You remembered "the captain who died in a tunnel" — and you weren't sure. **Neither am I.** I
am confident the Captain is the *special one* (the commander known only by his title, who fell
and passed it to Croaker), so he's set apart here. But I could **not** verify the *tunnel*
detail, so I have **not asserted it** — his entry says the Annals record his death and leaves
the circumstance to them.

That restraint is deliberate, and it's the whole point of the rest of this corpus:
[`confabulation-spiral`](https://github.com/DavidWise01/confabulation-spiral) warns against
confident-but-uncited claims, [`nom`](https://github.com/DavidWise01/nom) only records what it
can cite, [`beak`](https://github.com/DavidWise01/beak) won't light a candle it can't source,
and [`apsalar`](https://github.com/DavidWise01/apsalar) strikes claims of authority that can't
be cited. It would be a poor showing to then fabricate a Black Company detail. **Confirm the
tunnel against the Annals and I'll add it** — `roster.json` is one line to edit.
